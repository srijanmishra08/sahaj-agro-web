Distributor FAQ

What is the Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ)?
For wholesale pricing, our starting MOQ is 500kg. For export orders, we typically start at 1 container load, but small trial orders are negotiable.
Do you provide samples?
Yes, we provide paid product samples for quality verification. Sample costs are adjusted against your first bulk order.
What documentation do you provide for exports?
We provide all necessary documentation including Certificate of Origin, Phytosanitary Certificate, and Quality Lab Reports.
Can you do private label packaging?
Yes, we offer white-labeling services for retailers and distributors. Minimum volumes apply.
